About

The sole motive our platform is to connect the underrated/local chefs and the elite chefs of the metropolitan cities and to provide quality and hygiene maintained food and that too at home. After the pandemic everyone is so concerned about the safety of their own and the food they are eating. According to a survey food delivering apps are getting less number of orders placed these days as compared to the Era before the pandemic hitted all of us. When asked what is the reason for such a dip in the graph answer was the consumers/customers are worried about the hygiene of the food. They are also worried of placing food orders from the local big restaurants/food parlours. So to overcome this problem of hygiene and safety we thought of promoting chef culture in our country. A user can now hire a chef through the website or by our mobile application according to their ease. Customer can choose chefs according to their convenience just by tapping the hire option. The uniqueness about this culture is that First of all the food that is going to be prepared will be safe to eat and hygiene will be maintained. Also, the food can be customised according to the taste buds of the user and also it helps in cost cutting in the major parties where one has to order food from outside.
